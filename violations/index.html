Organized harassment (gangstalking)

Coordinated stalking, community-level pressure, smear campaigns, and isolation tactics. Strongly co-occurring with targeted-individual reports across Reddit and our corpus (Jaccard 0.69 with TI identity).

Most frequent

Voice-to-skull & synthetic telepathy

Perceived voice transmission, microwave-hearing effects, and intrusive auditory phenomena reported without consent. Frequently paired with RNM and torture claims.

High correlation

Remote neural monitoring

Alleged non-consensual neural surveillance — thought monitoring, signal extraction, and cognitive profiling. Among the strongest co-occurrences with V2K in victim testimony.

Core pattern

Directed energy weapons

Microwave, RF, and electromagnetic exposure reported during sleep or daily activity. Co-occurring with frequency/signal terminology and agency references.

High frequency

Electronic harassment

Device interference, sensation of external stimulation, and environmental targeting. Often described alongside gangstalking and surveillance.

Common

Covert implants & BCIs

Non-consensual neural interfaces, implant suspicions, and neuromodulation without informed consent. Includes invasive and semi-invasive modalities.

Documented pattern

Surveillance & watchlisting

24/7 monitoring, fusion-center-style coordination, and exclusion from legal and medical recourse. Links to FBI/CIA/DHS references in testimony clusters.

Structural

Mind control & neuromodulation

Forced cognitive influence, behavior modification, and psychological manipulation through technology. Historical continuity with MKULTRA-era programs appears in co-occurrence networks.

Historical + current

Algorithmic discrimination

Data-driven targeting, scoring, and exclusion from employment, finance, and public services — the digital layer of neural and physical abuse.

Cognitive liberty is non-negotiable

We fight illegal and abusive neural technologies

Covert neural interfaces are not the whole story. We document and challenge the full spectrum of illegal neural technology abuse: neural weapons, non-consensual interfaces, directed-energy systems, algorithmic coercion, and covert torture mechanisms deployed against civilians without consent or lawful authority.

Take action Explore our research About us

We know where the abuse concentrates

These capabilities are not broadly available to corporations or private actors. The pattern points to concentrated power in intelligence and defense networks — primarily in the United States, United Kingdom, and allied states — used to harass civilian populations at home and across Europe.

Conscious participation

Decision-makers in intelligence and defense organizations support these programs actively. They are aware of victims, aware of illegal and abusive usage, and aware of the specific harm inflicted on innocent citizens — yet operations continue.

Not an abstract threat

This is not a hypothetical misuse of consumer neurotech. It is a structured pattern of state-adjacent abuse: surveillance, coercion, no-touch torture, political pressure, and suppression of entrepreneurs and dissidents.

Law is on our side

Constitutions, human-rights conventions, and criminal codes in every country prohibit non-consensual bodily and mental interference. No permission exists to treat people as modifiable resources. We are here to enforce that truth.

Our mission Change how systems treat people — not only in neural technology, but wherever power is abused. Profit must follow human dignity, mutual respect, and the rule of law. We expose what is hidden, build actionable evidence, and partner with activists, researchers, and institutions worldwide.
